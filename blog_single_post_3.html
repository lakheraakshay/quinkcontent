Gender Inequality

Gender inequality is one of the major problems and root cause of many social evils that blossom in a patriarchal society such as female infanticide, no education for a girl child, and little or no right to women in any decision making in their families or society. To eradicate such social abuses with females it is important to attack its route cause, that is discrimination between males and females in societies. However, in rules and laws, discrimination have been abolished decades ago in almost every modern society, but the practical situation is an inversion to what we see in ideal law situations and other books. So to provide women equal status in patriarchal society I would suggest the following resolutions-

• Awareness - Although it seems like a typical heading in every article regarding any social topic, awareness is the most important and powerful aspect which has to power to bring in the change we are desiring to come. We have to educate people to aware them and make them think out of the circle they have been thinking about for ages. Educating people about the milestones women have achieved in various fields and their higher efficiency rate while doing any work will help people to change their outlook about girls, and they will start considering them a person rather than a burden.

• Exposure - Exposure and freedom are two necessities of a person if they want to succeed in any field. Thus we have to provide girls exposure to the outer world by letting them go into various fields of their choice and giving them the freedom to travel anywhere in the world where they are required to without any hesitation or second thought. Providing girls freedom at par with their male counterparts will help in improving growth chances for girls and when both males and females will get equal chances to succeed in any particular work their success rates would be similar to each other, thus leaving no reason for them to look the other as a burden or dumb.

• Reservation - This solution may not be liked by many people but according to me this can be a solution to instantaneously improve the female condition in any society because reservation not only helps in providing some extra odds to females for their success but also guarantees female representation in the concerning field. Thus people would have to give chances to females even if they don’t want them to be in higher positions. This will result in giving some powers to the hand of women thus allowing them to help other women to grow and stand in society. However in the long run reservation is not the best solution for the betterment of the weaker section of society, but it is one of the best immediate responses which helps in boosting the progress of discriminated sections.

Thus, by these resolutions, I can conclude that if the above measures are taken with a well-functioned plan the discrimination against women will slowly come to an end, and societies’ outlook will shift from patriarchal to that of neutral where all genders are equal.
