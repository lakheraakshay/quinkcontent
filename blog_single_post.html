Similarities and Differences between Canadian Hospitality Industry & Indian Hospitality Industry

There are lots of similarities in Hospitality Industry between both the regions but listing down some of them like Mumbai has a lot of national heritage sites bringing in history buffs, and a wide variety of nature reserves and parks for those looking for adventure while on the other hand Canada is also known for natural sights and national heritage mostly. In both the regions, there is a strong need to invite international travellers and tourists. The Hospitality Industry in both the regions is establishing well but is largely focused on local tourists. The issue with local tourists is that they do not stay for a longer time resulting in less revenue generation. It has been the practice of the hospitality industry in Canada & Mumbai both not to advertise through any sort of media due to its peculiar business nature. The Hospitality Industry, nowadays, is largely dependent on the online promotion and that is why many vertical and horizontal websites are continuously pursuing the need to cater hospitality industry in both the countries like in Canada there is Catchfree.ca. The differences are that the drivability and cheap flights make Canada an easy destination for vacationing US families. Additionally, US citizens can obtain a specific visa to visit Canada. It is easier to obtain than a regular passport which increases visitors from the US. In case of Mumbai, The citizens trying to visit have to obtain visa which makes visiting to Mumbai a difficult choice for travelers. Canada's largest draw is its natural sights.

"With places like Niagara Falls and other natural wonders, people come to experience Canada's nature. Skiing and shopping on French-inspired streets are also popular attractions, but nature is number one while India (Mumbai) is a country with a long history, and historically many people visit the country for spiritual reasons, bringing in visitors from all over the world."

Additionally, Mumbai has become recently popular for low-cost medical treatments. With highly skilled doctors and the cost for medical treatments so low, it brings in people from all over the world to have work done that they cannot afford at home even with insurance. Since medical procedures can take time, it ensures that 'medical tourists' stay longer, which adds significantly to the revenue of the country. The Indian government has actually realized the positive of this type of tourism and has created a new visa which is easier to obtain for these situations while in case of Canada people do not visit the country for medical treatments. The medical treatment over there is costly.
